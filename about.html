My Story

I've been passionate about competitive gaming for over 5 years. What started as a casual hobby transformed into a serious competitive pursuit, leading me to achieve Immortal rank in Valorant. Along the way, I discovered my passion for analyzing gameplay, understanding meta shifts, and helping others improve.

Through thousands of hours of gameplay, tournament competition, and content creation, I've learned valuable lessons about:

  • Advanced gameplay mechanics and positioning
  • Team coordination and communication strategies
  • Professional tournament meta and strategies
  • Building skills and climbing the competitive ladder
  • Monetizing gaming talent and building a personal brand

This blog is my way of sharing these insights with the Valorant community and helping players at all levels achieve their competitive goals.
